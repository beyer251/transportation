KNOW THE SIGNS
The following is a list of potential red flags and indicators of human trafficking to help you recognize the signs. If you see any of these red flags, contact the National Human Trafficking Hotline at 1-888-373-7888 for specialized victim services referrals or to report the situation.
The presence of these red flags is an indication that further assessment may be necessary to identify a potential human trafficking situation.
It is important to be alert and aware at all times when you are alone.
Signs of trafficking scouting include strangers approaching you while you’re alone, someone who doesn’t keep their story straight, and tries to convince you to come with them. They may show signs of aggressive behavior and put off my the mention of family near by. They may force you to take drugs or alcohol and make sexual moves on you without consent.
Never reveal private information to a stranger

If you sense you are being followed, call 911 immediately

If a stranger does approach you, let them know you have friends and family in the area

Allow your 3 closest friends or family to GPS track your phone

Don’t let anyone know where you live until you get to know them
